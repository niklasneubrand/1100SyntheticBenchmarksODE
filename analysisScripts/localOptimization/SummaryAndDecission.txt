Unfortunately none of the testet localOptimization approaches performed well on all problems. This is somehow expected, since some problems require looser or stricter tolerances than others.
The heuristioc approaches with tight optimization bounds or two-step (first dynamic then all parameters) did not perform sufficiently better than other methods. For simplicity in explaining the approach, I do not consider them further.
The very strict tolerance 1e-10 takes a huige amount of compute power. This is unpractical. We also do not consider it further.

This leaves us with two approaches V2 (tols at 1e-8) and V3 (tols at 1e-6).
